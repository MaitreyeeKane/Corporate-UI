Bhopal

Situated in Madhya Pradesh the State Capital Bhopal has an affluent and varied historical backdrop. The city is located north of the gorgeous Vindhya Mountain range and creates a periphery with its array of small hills. Bhopal today with its multi-faceted profile is an interesting place to visit.Known as the City of Lakes, it is a pleasing blend of tranquility and harmony. Located on a gradient, the metropolitan has a coliseum like eminence, with a fair dash of serene gardens and lakes.As we come close to the city, titanic minarets and mosques can be seen posturing as an emblematic indication of lake city . Bhopal has a widespread, well urbanized transportation network, which makes it reachable from diverse parts of the country.
